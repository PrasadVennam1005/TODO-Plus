# TODO++ v1.7.0 Showcase - YouTube Video Script

**Target Length:** ~2-3 Minutes
**Prep Work:** 
1. Open IntelliJ IDEA with the `presentation_samples` project loaded.
2. Have `BackendShowcase.kt` open in the editor.
3. Close the TODO++ tool window initially so you can reveal it during the video.

---

### 🎬 Scene 1: The Hook & Introduction (0:00 - 0:25)
**Visual:** Screen recording starts on your IDE. You are looking at regular code.
**Audio (You):** 
"Hey everyone! If you're tired of losing track of your `// TODO` comments, or forgetting when technical debt is due, you need to see the massive new v1.7.0 update for the TODO++ plugin. Today, I'm going to show you how to turn your basic code comments into a fully-fledged, cross-language project management system right inside IntelliJ."

### 🎬 Scene 2: Rich Metadata & The Tool Window (0:25 - 0:55)
**Visual:** 
1. Open the **TODO++** tool window at the bottom.
2. Click the **Scan** button. 
3. The Tree view populates. 
**Audio (You):**
"Here is the brand new Premium Tree UI. Instead of a flat, boring list, TODO++ parses rich metadata out of your comments. By simply adding parentheses, you can assign tasks using `@username`, set priorities like `CRITICAL` or `HIGH`, and even link directly to your Jira or GitHub issues like `PROJ-101`. Notice how it automatically color-codes the badges based on urgency!"

### 🎬 Scene 3: Grouping & Multi-Language Support (0:55 - 1:25)
**Visual:** 
1. Click the **Group By** dropdown in the toolbar. Change it to **Priority**, then change it to **Assignee**.
2. Click on the `DatabaseShowcase.sql` file or `FrontendShowcase.js` to show that it works everywhere.
**Audio (You):**
"With the new Grouping feature, you can instantly organize hundreds of tasks by Priority, Assignee, or File. And the best part? It works across your entire stack. Whether it's Kotlin, Java, Javascript, CSS, or even SQL `-- TODO` comments, the scanner catches everything and aggregates it into one unified dashboard."

### 🎬 Scene 4: Due Dates & Quick Fixes (1:25 - 2:00)
**Visual:** 
1. Point to an overdue task in the UI (highlighted in red).
2. Go to the editor, type a basic `// TODO: fix this bug`. 
3. Press `Alt+Enter` (or `Option+Enter`), click the lightbulb, and select **Upgrade to TODO++ format**.
4. On a new line, type `todo+` and press `Tab` to show the Live Template.
**Audio (You):**
"It even tracks due dates, turning red when a task is overdue! And adopting it is seamless. Got old, basic TODOs? Just press `Alt+Enter` and the Quick Fix Intention will instantly upgrade it to the rich format for you. Or, use the new `todo+` Live Template—just type `todo+`, hit `Tab`, and it auto-fills your username and priority automatically."

### 🎬 Scene 5: HTML Export Dashboard (2:00 - 2:25)
**Visual:** 
1. Go back to the Tool Window. 
2. Click the **Export** icon and select **Export HTML Dashboard**. 
3. Save it, and let Chrome pop open to show the beautiful dark-mode web dashboard.
**Audio (You):**
"Finally, for your daily standups or team meetings, just click Export to HTML Dashboard. TODO++ generates a stunning, offline-ready web report with priority charts and sortable metrics, right in your browser. No extra setup required."

### 🎬 Scene 6: Outro (2:25 - 2:40)
**Visual:** Show the JetBrains Marketplace page for TODO++, or have the plugin settings page open.
**Audio (You):**
"TODO++ v1.7.0 is officially live right now. Go to Settings > Plugins in your JetBrains IDE, search for TODO++, and turbocharge your workflow today. Let me know what you think in the comments below!"
